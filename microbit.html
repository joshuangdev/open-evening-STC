Rock Paper Scissors on Microbit

Press left for ROCK, right for PAPER, left and right for SCISSORS.

To play the game, simply press either (A which is LEFT), (B which is RIGHT) or (A+B which is left and right at the same time).

If you and the computer choose the same thing, it's a tie.

If you choose rock and your computer chooses scissors, you win!

If you choose scissors and your computer chooses paper, you win!

If you choose paper and your computer chooses rock, you win!

Have fun playing Rock Paper Scissors on your Microbit!
