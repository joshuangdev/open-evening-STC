Rock Paper Scissors on Microbit

This is a simple HTML page that describes how to play Rock Paper Scissors on a Microbit.

To play the game, simply shake your Microbit. The Microbit will randomly choose either rock, paper, or scissors.

If you and your opponent choose the same thing, it's a tie.

If you choose rock and your opponent chooses scissors, you win!

If you choose scissors and your opponent chooses paper, you win!

If you choose paper and your opponent chooses rock, you win!

Have fun playing Rock Paper Scissors on your Microbit!
